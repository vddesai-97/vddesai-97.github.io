Yesterday was a day of rest. No errands. No worrying. No fixing. No arguing. A day to exist. To be present. Nothing more. During this time, Clark and I had some lengthy conversations about our future and what the could look like. One of the topics that came up was the idea of investing in yourself. For me, I've had this idea to start a research company to develop and use a model that would be beneficial to millions of people. But that requires spending money on myself while I apply and try to get funding. But as two people who ar unemployed and traveling full-time, spending more money on us for a possible future is scary. But believing in one's self is even scarier. I think I'm going to do it though. Believe in myself. Believe in my partner. Invest and see what happens.
